Chow

Cool locally sourced creative menu. Always a line after 9:30 am. Nice outdoor
seating, cute cottage style indoor seating but can be loud. Serves breakfast
and lunch. Eclectic and organic style food. Has outdoor seating tucked away
from the road, allows dogs.

McKay Cottage

Great bloody mary's, cottage style indoor seating that can be a little cramped.
Usually pretty busy with a line out the door. Serves breakfast and lunch. Usually
pretty large and hearty portions. More homestyle or traditional food. Has some
outdoor seating, unknown if dogs are allowed.
