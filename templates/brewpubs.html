GoodLife

GoodLife has several solid IPA'a and pale ales, as well as a great Bavarian lager.
Standard pub fare menu done well, but lacking in gluten free or vegetarian options.
GoodLife is on the Westside of Bend, with easy parking. They have an indoor setting
that is relatively small, but has a few large tables for big groups. In addition, they
have two large outdoor seating areas, one on a paved patio in between the
buildings, and another on a large lawn tucked behind the brewery. The Biergarten
has a large firepit, and umbrellas at all the tables to help with the sun. The
patio has small fires to keep you warm in the winter as well. They have a full bar,
and sell beer and liquor to go. With a locals night on Thursdays for 3$ pints,
and the large dog-friendly lawn, GoodLife is one you won't want to miss.
