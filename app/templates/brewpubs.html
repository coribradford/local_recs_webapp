GoodLife Brewing

GoodLife has several solid IPA'a and pale ales, as well as a great Bavarian lager. Standard pub fare menu done well, but lacking in gluten free or vegetarian options. GoodLife is on the Westside of Bend, with easy parking. They have an indoor setting that is relatively small, but has a few large tables for big groups. In addition, they have two large outdoor seating areas, one on a paved patio in between the buildings, and another on a large lawn tucked behind the brewery. The Biergarten has a large firepit, and umbrellas at all the tables to help with the sun. The patio has small fires to keep you warm in the winter as well. They have a full bar, and sell beer and liquor to go. With a locals night on Thursdays for 3$ pints, and the large dog-friendly lawn, GoodLife is one you won't want to miss. Website Map

Bevel Craft Brewing

Bevel Brewing has several great IPA'a and pale ales, with a solid sour every now and then. They have a food truck pod in lieu of a kitchen, with four trucks. For more info on the trucks themselves, check out the food truck page. Located on the Southeast side of Bend, Bevel usually has easy parking, but they can get crowded on a sunny Friday afternoon. There are a few tables inside, and a large outdoor setting with some gravel and some pavement. They have umbrellas at almost every table, and a large awesome fire for the winter that has limited seating around it. Dogs are welcome, and they provide bowls of water, but the gravel and pavement can be a little uncomfortable for them. They does crowler and growler fills, but doesn't have six-packs for sale. Bevel is also the local disc-golfer's bar, with discs for sale and professional disc-golf on the tv's. Somewhat off the beaten path, with nice flowers, and tucked away from the road, Bevel is a great spot for beers and food. Website Map
